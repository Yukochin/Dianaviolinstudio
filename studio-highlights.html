Studio Highlights

Students from Diana Violin Studio have received recognition for their artistic development and commitment across both local and international stages. Their experiences span performances, competitions, and collaborations that reflect the studio’s emphasis on growth, expression, and musicianship.

International Collaborations & Recognition

United Kingdom · Ongoing

University College London Orchestra (UCL Orchestra)

Collaborations include chamber tours, Arts Fair events, solo features, and recording projects across the UK — giving our musicians exposure to professional standards and ensemble culture.

Korea · Sungshin University

National Music Competition — 3rd Prize (Violin)

Recognition by the Sungshin University College of Music and the Sungshin University National Music Competition, where a student received 3rd Prize in Violin.

London Young Musician (LYM)

Over Twenty Prizes & Teacher Award (2023)

Our young musicians earned distinctions across categories — Gold, Silver, Bronze, and a Special Prize for Engaging Performance. In 2023, Diana Violin Studio received the London Young Musician Teacher Award for its meaningful, holistic music education.

Community & School Stages

Beyond competitions, students remain active within their schools and communities, performing in solo and chamber ensembles. These experiences continue to cultivate confidence, collaboration, and stage presence — qualities that embody the spirit of the studio’s teaching philosophy.
